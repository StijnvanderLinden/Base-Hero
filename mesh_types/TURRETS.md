# Turrets

## Gameplay Purpose

Turrets are mechanical defense units that help the player hold the base. Their role must be readable at a glance.

## Visual Identity

- Handcrafted mechanical defense machines
- Toy-like engineered props
- Chunky bases and clear weapon heads
- Powered by simple mechanisms or blue energy cores

## Shape Language

- Strong base footprint
- Clear aiming direction
- Exaggerated barrel, launcher, lens, or emitter
- Rotating head visually distinct from base
- Support struts and mechanical pivots

## Required Components

- Ground base
- Rotating head or weapon mount
- Barrel, launcher, lens, or emitter
- Energy core or mechanical power source
- Simple support structure

## Do

- Make turret role readable by silhouette.
- Keep barrels thick enough to read from distance.
- Use blue glow for powered elements.
- Separate base and head if rotation is needed.
- Put pivot at footprint center.
- Put turret head origin at rotation point if exported separately.

## Don't

- Make turrets sleek sci-fi.
- Hide the weapon function inside tiny detail.
- Add too many barrels unless it communicates role.
- Use thin fragile supports.
- Make every turret the same shape with different colors only.

## Role Guidelines

### Fast Shooter

- Multiple small barrels or a spinning mechanism
- Compact frame
- Light, quick silhouette
- Small blue energy core or gear cluster

### Heavy Cannon

- One large barrel
- Heavy base
- Braced supports
- Big recoil shape

### AoE Turret

- Round emitter, mortar bowl, or pulse dish
- Broad head shape
- Energy chamber or explosive canister
- Larger visual radius cues

### Sniper Turret

- Long barrel or lens
- Tall stabilizing stand
- Narrow forward silhouette
- Clear precision shape

## Variation Ideas

- Stone thrower turret
- Brass repeater turret
- Energy pulse tower
- Heavy cannon nest
- Long-range crystal lens turret
