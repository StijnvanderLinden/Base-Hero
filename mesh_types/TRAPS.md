# Traps

## Gameplay Purpose

Traps are ground-based mechanical devices that punish enemies entering an area. Their function should be readable before activation.

## Visual Identity

- Mechanical floor devices
- Clear trigger area
- Chunky, built, engineered
- Dangerous but readable

## Shape Language

- Low profile
- Strong footprint silhouette
- Visible hazard element
- Clear active/inactive state potential

## Required Components

- Base plate
- Trigger or pressure element
- Hazard component
- Optional energy indicator
- Ground contact

## Do

- Make the trap footprint obvious.
- Use shape to communicate effect.
- Keep height low enough for path readability.
- Use simple moving parts where possible.
- Put origin at center of footprint.

## Don't

- Make traps visually blend into the floor.
- Use tiny spikes that disappear from camera distance.
- Add complex mechanisms that obscure function.
- Make traps look like resource props.

## Variation Ideas

- Spike plate
- Gear grinder
- Spring hammer
- Energy pulse tile
- Slow field emitter
- Flame/steam vent

## Example Roles

- Damage trap: spikes, blades, crusher
- Slow trap: blue field, sticky mechanical pads
- Burst trap: spring-loaded hammer or explosive core
- Control trap: clamp, snare, magnetic anchor
