# Gear And Weapons

## Gameplay Purpose

Gear and weapons communicate player actions, enemy threats, and upgrade identity. They must read clearly in motion.

## Visual Identity

- Exaggerated, chunky, functional
- Engineer-tech fantasy
- Built from wood, metal, stone, leather, and blue energy
- Simple enough to identify instantly

## Shape Language

- Large primary silhouette
- Clear handle/grip area
- Clear striking, firing, or functional end
- Thick proportions
- Minimal small decoration

## Required Components

For melee weapons:

- Blade/head
- Guard or transition piece
- Grip
- Pommel/end cap
- Clear swing pivot

For ranged weapons:

- Barrel/emitter
- Grip/body
- Energy or ammo element
- Clear firing direction

## Do

- Align weapon to a predictable axis.
- Keep the grip connected to the weapon body.
- Make function readable from silhouette.
- Use bevels on hard edges.
- Make all parts touch or overlap cleanly.
- Put origin at grip or swing pivot.

## Don't

- Leave blade, grip, guard, or pommel floating.
- Make weapons too thin.
- Add tiny ornamental detail.
- Use generic realistic medieval style without engineered identity.
- Make sci-fi guns with sleek panels.

## Variation Ideas

- Stone sword
- Hammer-wrench
- Gear axe
- Crystal-tipped spear
- Scrap cannon
- Energy staff
- Repeater crossbow

## Example Roles

- Melee cleave weapon
- Heavy stagger weapon
- Fast ranged weapon
- Burst ranged weapon
- Utility builder tool
