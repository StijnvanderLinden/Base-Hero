# Player Character

## Gameplay Purpose

The player character is the active defender who fights alongside base defenses. The silhouette must remain readable during combat and co-op movement.

## Visual Identity

- Stylized humanoid
- Practical engineer-adventurer
- Simple gear
- Heroic but not overly detailed
- Built for action and base defense

## Shape Language

- Clear head/body/limb separation
- Slightly exaggerated hands, boots, or shoulder gear
- Chunky readable equipment
- Strong weapon silhouette
- Neutral proportions that animate cleanly

## Required Components

- Head
- Torso
- Arms
- Legs
- Simple boots/gloves
- Gear belt or backpack
- Weapon attachment point

## Do

- Keep the silhouette readable from medium distance.
- Use simple gear to show the player as a builder/fighter.
- Make weapons oversized enough to read.
- Keep forms animation-friendly.
- Use team color or player marker separately from body detail.

## Don't

- Add realistic facial detail.
- Use thin limbs that vanish in motion.
- Overload the character with tiny tools.
- Make the player look futuristic sci-fi.
- Make gear obscure weapon readability.

## Variation Ideas

- Engineer tunic
- Stone-age builder gear
- Brass tool belt
- Light shoulder guard
- Backpack power core
- Simple helmet/goggles

## Example Roles

- Base defender
- Builder/fighter hybrid
- Melee/ranged weapon user
- Co-op readable teammate
